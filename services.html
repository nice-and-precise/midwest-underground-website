Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD)

Horizontal Directional Drilling represents the pinnacle of modern underground utility installation. Our advanced HDD technology allows us to install underground utilities, fiber optic cables, and conduit systems with surgical precision while preserving the integrity of roads, landscaping, and existing infrastructure. This trenchless method minimizes environmental impact, reduces project timelines, and delivers cost-effective solutions for even the most challenging installations.

With three decades of directional drilling experience, our team has mastered the art and science of underground boring. We utilize state-of-the-art guidance systems, powerful drilling rigs capable of handling bores from 50 feet to several thousand feet, and sophisticated tracking technology to ensure millimeter-accurate placement. Whether you're installing fiber optic networks under busy highways, placing utilities beneath rivers and wetlands, or navigating complex urban environments, our HDD expertise delivers reliable results without the disruption of traditional trenching methods.

Key Capabilities

  • Bore lengths from 50 feet to 3,000+ feet with pinpoint accuracy
  • Diameter capabilities from 2 inches to 24 inches for various applications
  • Real-time GPS tracking and electronic guidance systems for precision placement
  • Minimal surface disruption - preserve roads, landscaping, and existing infrastructure
  • Environmental compliance for wetlands, waterways, and protected areas

Equipment & Technology

  • State-of-the-art directional drill rigs (multiple size classes)
  • Advanced locating and tracking equipment for underground navigation
  • Horizontal boring equipment for various soil conditions
  • Specialized tooling for rock, clay, sand, and mixed soil formations
  • Vacuum excavation systems for safe potholing and utility location

Typical Applications

  • Fiber optic cable installation for broadband networks
  • Highway, railroad, and waterway crossings
  • Telecommunications conduit placement
  • Power line and electrical conduit installation
  • Water, sewer, and gas line placement

Underground Utilities Installation

Underground utilities form the invisible infrastructure that powers modern life. From water and sewer lines to electrical conduits and natural gas distribution, Midwest Underground delivers comprehensive utility installation services for residential developments, commercial projects, municipal infrastructure, and industrial facilities. Our expertise spans the full spectrum of underground utility work, combining proven installation methods with modern technology to ensure reliable, long-lasting results.

We understand that utility installation requires more than just digging trenches. Underground Geothermal Systems

Geothermal heating and cooling represents one of the most energy-efficient and environmentally sustainable HVAC solutions available today. Midwest Underground specializes in the installation of underground geothermal loop fields - the critical infrastructure that enables these high-efficiency systems to harness the earth's stable underground temperatures for year-round climate control. Our expertise in horizontal directional drilling and precision excavation makes us the ideal partner for both residential and commercial geothermal installations.

Geothermal system installation requires specialized knowledge of underground conditions, proper loop design, and precise installation techniques. Our team works closely with HVAC contractors and system designers to install horizontal loop fields, vertical bore systems, and pond loops that maximize efficiency and longevity. We handle everything from site preparation and loop field excavation to pipe placement, fusion welding, pressure testing, and complete restoration. Whether you're building a new energy-efficient home or retrofitting a commercial building with sustainable heating and cooling, our geothermal installation services deliver the underground infrastructure needed for decades of reliable operation.

Installation Services

  • Horizontal loop field excavation and installation
  • Vertical bore hole drilling for closed-loop systems
  • Pond and lake loop installations for water-source systems
  • HDPE pipe fusion welding and pressure testing
  • Complete loop field pressure testing and system commissioning

System Types & Applications

  • Residential geothermal heating and cooling systems
  • Commercial building energy-efficient HVAC installations
  • Large-scale institutional and industrial geothermal systems
  • New construction and retrofit applications
  • Coordination with HVAC contractors and system designers

Benefits of Geothermal Systems

  • Up to 70% reduction in heating and cooling costs
  • Environmentally sustainable with minimal carbon footprint
  • Quiet operation with no outdoor equipment noise
  • Extremely long system lifespan (25+ years for underground loops)
  • Consistent year-round performance in Minnesota's climate
