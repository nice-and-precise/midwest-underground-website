Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Willmar, MN and primarily serve Kandiyohi County and the central Minnesota region. For larger projects, we can extend our service area throughout Minnesota. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes! We provide free, no-obligation consultations and quotes for all projects. Simply fill out our contact form or give us a call at (320) 382-6636, and we'll schedule a time to assess your project and provide a detailed estimate.

How quickly can you start a project?

Project timelines vary based on scope, permitting requirements, and our current schedule. Emergency services are available immediately 24/7. For planned projects, we typically can begin within 1-2 weeks after approval, though large municipal or commercial projects may require more lead time for permitting.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. Midwest Underground of Minnesota is a fully licensed Technology Systems Contractor with comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. We maintain all required state and local licenses and certifications for underground utility work.

Can you handle emergency utility repairs?

Yes. We offer 24/7 emergency services for fiber breaks, utility line damage, and urgent underground infrastructure repairs. Our emergency response team can be on-site quickly to assess the situation and begin repairs immediately to minimize downtime.

What information do you need for a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we need details about your project including: the type of service needed (HDD, fiber installation, utilities, etc.), project location, approximate scope or length of work, timeline requirements, and any site-specific challenges. Photos or site plans are helpful but not required for initial quotes.

Ready to Get Started?

Our team of underground utilities experts is ready to help you plan and execute your next project. Contact us today to discuss your needs.
