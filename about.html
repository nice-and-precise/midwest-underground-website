Our Story: Three Decades of Growth

In 1991, Midwest Underground of Minnesota Inc was founded on a simple principle: do underground work right the first time. What began as a small operation in Willmar has grown into central Minnesota's most trusted name in directional drilling, fiber optic installation, and underground utilities.

Over the past 34 years, we've witnessed remarkable changes in our industry. We started when horizontal directional drilling was still considered cutting-edge technology, and we've continuously invested in the latest equipment and training to stay ahead of the curve. From our early days installing basic utility lines to today's complex fiber optic networks supporting Minnesota's broadband expansion, we've evolved alongside the infrastructure needs of our communities.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to the values that built this company. Safety remains our top priority on every job site. Quality craftsmanship guides every bore, every splice, every installation. And our relationships with clients, built on trust and reliability, continue to be the foundation of our success.

Today, our team of 18 skilled professionals brings centuries of combined experience to projects across Kandiyohi County and beyond. We've installed thousands of miles of underground utilities, navigated countless complex drilling challenges, and helped build the infrastructure that powers homes, businesses, and communities throughout central Minnesota. As Minnesota invests $651 million in broadband expansion, we're proud to be at the forefront, bringing high-speed internet to underserved areas with the same precision and care we've always delivered.

We're not just contractors‚Äîwe're your neighbors, community members, and partners in building Minnesota's future, one careful bore at a time.

Our Journey: 34 Years of Milestones

From a small operation to central Minnesota's trusted underground infrastructure expert‚Äîhere's how we've grown.

1991

Company Founded

Midwest Underground of Minnesota Inc established in Willmar with a focus on precision underground utilities installation. Started with basic directional drilling equipment and a commitment to quality.

1997

Fleet Expansion

Invested in additional HDD rigs and support equipment, doubling our capacity. Began taking on larger municipal and commercial projects across Kandiyohi County.

2003

Fiber Optic Specialization

Recognized growing telecommunications demand and invested heavily in fiber optic installation capabilities. Trained team in splicing, testing, and network deployment.

2010

Technology Systems Contractor License

Obtained Minnesota Technology Systems Contractor License, expanding our official capabilities and qualifying for advanced telecommunications infrastructure projects.

2015

Safety Program Enhancement

Implemented comprehensive safety management system with full-time safety director. Achieved milestone of zero lost-time incidents through enhanced training and protocols.

2020

Equipment Modernization

Major equipment upgrade including state-of-the-art HDD rigs with advanced locating systems. Invested in GPS-guided drilling technology for unprecedented accuracy.

2023

Broadband Expansion Leadership

Became key contractor supporting Minnesota's $651 million broadband expansion initiative. Partnered with multiple telecommunications providers to bring high-speed internet to underserved central Minnesota communities.
